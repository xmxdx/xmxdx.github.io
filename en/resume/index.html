Projects & Collaborations

  • 2019

    Forever Do Game, w/ Jill Townsley [JRC, Ispra]

    ABOUT

    ROLE

    Designed and developed the system to track all the individual elements (boxes) and to visually represent their movement paths with lines which faded with time.

  • 2019

    Nidra, w/ Otolab [Milan]

    ABOUT

    Audiovisual live performance that intends to lead the audience through various states of consciousness by means of an accurate mix of binaural beats and spectral modulation. As a representation of the multiple elements that compose the sound, several sources of concentric circles expand, weave together and give rise to elaborate textures.

    ROLE

    Developed the code that generates the audio and the visuals.

    ABOUT

    ROLE

  • 2017

    Tremors [Goldsmiths University, London]

    ABOUT

    Electronic musical instrument involving several lasers that read the perturbations on the surface of a liquid and translate them into an immersive 7-channels spatialised sound.

    ROLE

    Designed and developed the code, the electronics and the physical structure.

  • 2013

    Punto Zero, w/ Otolab [Milan]

    ABOUT

  • 2013

    Mnemorphosis, w/ Otolab [Milan]

    ABOUT
